How to Play

Goal: Move all 7 pieces off the board.

Move: Roll 4 dice. Count white tips (0-4).

Rosettes: Flower tiles give an extra turn + safety.

Capture: Land on opponent in middle row to send them back.

Exact Roll: Required to exit.
